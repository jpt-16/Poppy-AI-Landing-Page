Questions? Answered.

How is Poppy different from ChatGPT or Claude?
ChatGPT and Claude are both inside Poppy — plus Gemini and Grok. The difference is the canvas: instead of pasting context into a chat box, you drop whole videos, PDFs, and voice notes on a board and every model reads all of it at once. You also pay for one plan instead of stacking four separate subscriptions — or skip renewals entirely with the lifetime option.
Will the output actually sound like me?
Yes — Poppy learns your voice from your own content and remembers it across every board. Set it once during onboarding; everything stays on-brand automatically.
I'm not technical. How long until I get value?
Onboarding reads your brand and builds your first board in minutes — and you get a free 1-on-1 call where the Poppy team builds a board for your exact use case in under 45 minutes.
What if it's not for me?
30-day money-back guarantee — to stay eligible, book and attend an onboarding call with our team within your first 30 days. If you skip that call, you forfeit refund eligibility. See our Refund Policy for full terms.
Can my team use it?
Yes — boards are multiplayer, Figma-style. Writers, editors, and strategists work on the same canvas at the same time.
What is Poppy AI?
Founder here ❤️ Poppy AI can summarize videos in different languages, and it's great for teachers building lesson plans. It's a paid product — we're bootstrapped, no outside funding. And yes, excuse the website design — we're devs, not designers 😛
What AI is it using?
ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and Grok — all four, in one place.
Do I have to be a paid user of ChatGPT?
Nope — you get ChatGPT and Claude in here. You can cancel those subscriptions ❤️
Is there a free trial?
No free trial — since we're bootstrapped (no funding 😅), this is how we do it. But we give you a 30-day money-back guarantee.
Can you upload video instead of using YouTube links?
Yes! You can drag and drop downloaded videos straight into your Poppy board. MP3 and MP4 files work too 🚀
What about TikTok videos and YouTube Shorts?
TikTok, yes. YouTube, yes. Instagram Reels, yes.
Can it create mind maps and flow charts?
Mind maps are in beta (it breaks right now) — no flow charts yet.
Can Poppy create images or videos?
Can't create images or videos yet.
How does it compare to Blaze.ai?
It's way better than Blaze — basically Blaze but with way more features, minus the social media scheduler.
Is there an app you can use on your phone?
No app yet! Desktop only for now.
